Pay Online

Welcome to the Town of Cedar Fort Online Payment Portal. Here you can pay your town utility bill online using a credit card or PayPal. To ensure that your payment is credited to your account, it is important that you read and perform the following steps.

  • Enter the amount shown on your utility bill.**
  • Enter the account number that appears on your utility bill.
  • Enter the name that appears on your utility bill.
  • Press the Pay Now Button.
  • You will then be taken to the payment portal.
  • Select whether you will pay by credit card or log in to your PayPal account, then follow the instructions to complete the transaction.
